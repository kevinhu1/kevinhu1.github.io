Intern Paradise is a platform that helps local companies in Hawai ªi find potential interns. The current method of finding interns is time intensive and does not provide enough exposure to college students. Intern Paradise is a website designed for companies to post their internship positions, and students, no matter what major they are in or college they go to, can apply.

Sole graphic designer in a team of five, creating user experience products including site maps, information architecture, wireframes, and prototypes for a user-end startup product. Conceptualized all design aspects of business, including logo, student-company differentiation, iconography. Essense of design and business focuses on weakness of competitors including InternMatch.com and Internships.com.
